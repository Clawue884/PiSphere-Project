# Participating in Governance

As a token holder, you have a voice in the governance of PiSphere.

## Proposing Changes
1. Navigate to the "Governance" section of the platform.
2. Click on "Create Proposal" and fill in the required details.
3. Submit your proposal for review.

## Voting on Proposals
1. Go to the "Active Proposals" section.
2. Review the proposals and click "Vote" on the one you wish to support.
3. Confirm your vote.

## Tracking Proposal Outcomes
You can track the status of your proposals and votes in the "Governance" section.
